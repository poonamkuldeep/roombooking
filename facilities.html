OUR FACILITIES

Our hotel rooms offer modern comfort with a cozy bed, air conditioning, free Wi-Fi,
flat-screen TV, and a private bathroom. Guests can enjoy room service, complimentary
toiletries, and a minibar. You'll also have access to our on-site gym, restaurant, and
free parking for a relaxing and convenient stay.

Wi-Fi

Enjoy complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi in every hotel room, perfect for staying connected,
streaming, or getting work done during your stay. Reliable internet access is available
throughout the hotel for your convenience.

Food

Our hotel offers a variety of dining options to satisfy every taste. Guests can enjoy a complimentary
breakfast buffet, featuring fresh local and international dishes. Room service is available for in-room dining,
and our on-site restaurant serves lunch and dinner with a diverse menu. Special dietary requirements are accommodated,
ensuring a delightful dining experience for all.

Free Parking

Enjoy the convenience of complimentary parking for all guests during your stay. Our secure parking facility is easily
accessible, providing peace of mind while you explore the hotel and surrounding areas.
